---
title: "Glossary"
description: "Definitions for protocol and integration terms"
---

- `ML-KEM-768`: NIST-standard KEM used for encapsulation/decapsulation.
- `Meta-address`: Serialized recipient identity containing spending and viewing public keys.
- `View tag`: One-byte filter derived from shared secret for cheap scan filtering.
- `Announcement`: Published record containing ephemeral ciphertext and metadata.
- `Stealth address`: One-time derived recipient address.
- `ENS text record`: Key-value record used for `specter -> ipfs://CID` mapping.
- `SuiNS content hash`: Name record field holding content pointer for meta-address data.
- `CID`: Content identifier used by IPFS.
