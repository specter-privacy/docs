## Where stealth addresses go next

Private receiving is the foundation. Here's what it enables beyond basic payments.

## Upcoming use cases

### Private prediction markets

Bet on outcomes without broadcasting your positions. Stealth addresses keep your participation invisible to other market participants and on-chain observers.

### Private payroll

Organizations pay contributors to stealth addresses. No employee can see what others earn. HR or finance holds the meta-addresses; the actual pay destinations are unique per period.

### Multi-chain expansion

SPECTER already supports Ethereum and Sui. Coming next:

- **Arbitrum** - L2 stealth payments with lower gas costs
- **Base** - Same privacy guarantees on Coinbase's L2
- **Optimism** - Expanding the rollup support

The same meta-address works across all chains. One key setup, receive privately everywhere.

### Cross-chain stealth transfers

Send from Ethereum, receive privately on Sui (or vice versa). The ML-KEM shared secret can derive valid addresses for any chain, making cross-chain private receiving possible.

### Private subscriptions

Recurring stealth payments where each period goes to a new address. The service provider can verify payment through the announcement system without revealing the subscriber's identity on-chain.
